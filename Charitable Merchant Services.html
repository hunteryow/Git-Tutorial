About Us

Charitable Merchant Services is a leading provider of cost-effective credit card processing solutions. Our mission is to help businesses save money on their credit card processing fees while making a positive impact on their communities.

Through our innovative program, a portion of your credit card processing fees is donated to either your church or a charity of your choice each month. It's a win-win situation, where you save money and support causes you care about.

Our Services

Credit Card Processing

We offer competitive rates and advanced payment solutions to streamline your business transactions. With our secure and reliable processing services, you can accept credit and debit card payments with ease.

Donation Program

Our unique donation program allows you to give back to your community effortlessly. By partnering with us, a percentage of your credit card processing fees will be donated to a charity of your choice, making a meaningful impact in the lives of those in need.

Cost Savings

We understand the importance of cost savings for businesses. Our transparent pricing structure ensures that you receive competitive rates and save money on your credit card processing fees, allowing you to invest in the growth and success of your business.
